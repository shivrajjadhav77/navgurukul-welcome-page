About NavGurukul

NavGurukul is a non-profit organization on a mission to provide free, high-quality residential education in software engineering and life skills to underprivileged youth across India.

We believe that education should be a right, not a privilege — and that anyone with the passion to learn can build a better future for themselves and their communities.

What We Do

1-Year Residential Program

Students live, learn, and grow in a supportive campus environment.

Software Engineering

Learn programming, web development, problem-solving, and more.

Life Skills & Growth

Build confidence, communication skills, and emotional intelligence.

Women in Tech

Special campuses and support systems for female students.

Career Support

90%+ students get placed in top companies and startups.
